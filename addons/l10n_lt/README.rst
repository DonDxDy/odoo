Lithuania - Accounting
======================

Chart of Accounts (COA) Template for Lithuania's Accounting.

This module also includes:

* List of available banks in Lithuania.
* Tax groups.
* Most common Lithuanian Taxes.
* Fiscal positions.
* Account Tags.
